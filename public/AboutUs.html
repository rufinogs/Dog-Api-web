About Us



Welcome to Adopt Me, your number one source for all things adopting dogs.
We're dedicated to giving you the very best of service possible, with a focus on respect on the animal.


Founded in 2021 by The Lion Team, Adopt Me has come a long way from its beginnings in Krakow. When Rufi and Arthur first started out, their passion for taking care of dogs drove them to making this website so that Adopt Me can offer you the best service to adopt a dog online. We hope you enjoy our websiteas much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us with the contact us button.

Sincerely. The Lion Team
